Why Academy

Learn STEM by deriving it yourself.

A free, open-source platform that teaches physics and math through a derive-it-yourself methodology. Explore interactive simulations, predict outcomes, derive equations by hand on a freeform canvas, implement them in code, and explain them in your own words — every step verified deterministically by SymPy. No AI grading. No paywall.
